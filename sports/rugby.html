The Discovery of Rugby Photography

Coming to rugby without preconceptions has allowed me to see the sport purely through a photographer's lens. Every match reveals new visual stories - the way players support each other in rucks, the precise timing required for lineout throws, the split-second decisions that separate successful attacks from defensive stands. My soccer experience helps me understand field positioning and continuous flow, but rugby's unique elements constantly surprise and challenge me in the best possible ways.

The sport's emphasis on respect and fair play, evident in how players interact with referees and opponents during the match itself, creates authentic moments that showcase rugby's character beyond just the athletic competition. These glimpses of sportsmanship during intense competition offer compelling photographic opportunities that capture what makes rugby culture distinctive.
