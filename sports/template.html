The Beautiful Game in Motion

Soccer is pure energy in motion - the sprint down the sideline, the perfectly timed slide tackle, the celebration after a game-winning goal. As both a former collegiate soccer player and experienced sports photographer serving Richmond, Henrico, Chesterfield, Short Pump, Glen Allen, and Hanover, I understand the rhythm of the game and know exactly when those decisive moments happen.

Having played competitively through college, including serving as team captain and leading scorer, I bring an insider's perspective to every match I photograph. I know where to position myself for that crucial penalty kick, when a player is about to make their move, and how to capture the raw emotion that makes soccer photography so compelling.

  • Richmond Private School Specialist: Extensive experience photographing athletics at St. Christopher's, St. Catherine's, and Collegiate School, with additional event coverage at Trinity Episcopal, Steward School, Benedictine, and Veritas campuses.
