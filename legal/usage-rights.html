Image Usage Rights

Last Updated: June 2024

Personal Usage Rights

When you purchase images from Z Photography, you receive the following personal usage rights:

  • Printing for personal use (home display, gifts)
  • Sharing on personal social media accounts
  • Creating personal photo albums and books
  • Using as profile pictures and avatars

Commercial Usage Rights

Commercial usage of images requires additional licensing. This includes:

  • Business marketing materials
  • Website and social media for businesses
  • Print advertising
  • Product packaging
  • Corporate publications

Please contact us for commercial licensing inquiries.

Restrictions

The following uses are not permitted without explicit written permission:

  • Editing or altering the images
  • Removing watermarks or copyright notices
  • Reselling or redistributing the images
  • Using images for commercial purposes without proper licensing
  • Using images in a way that could be considered defamatory or inappropriate

Image Modifications

To maintain the quality and integrity of our work, we request that you:

  • Do not apply filters or effects to the images
  • Do not crop images in a way that changes their composition
  • Do not remove or alter any metadata
  • Contact us if you need any modifications to the images

Attribution

When sharing images online, we appreciate if you:

  • Credit Z Photography when possible
  • Tag us on social media (@zphotography)
  • Link back to our website when appropriate
