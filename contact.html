Frequently Asked Questions

How far do you travel for events?

I serve the entire Richmond metro area and central Virginia including all surrounding counties and communities: Henrico County (Short Pump, Glen Allen, Innsbrook), Chesterfield County (Midlothian, Bon Air), Hanover County (Mechanicsville, Ashland), plus Powhatan, Goochland, the West End, East End, North Side, South Side, and downtown Richmond. For tournaments or special events outside central Virginia, I'm happy to discuss travel arrangements.

What's included in sports coverage?

Every package includes comprehensive game coverage, professional editing with my signature bright and energetic style, online gallery delivery within 3-5 business days, and personal usage rights for families. College recruitment usage is always included.

Can you photograph multiple sports in one season?

Absolutely. Many families book season packages across different sports, and I offer discounts for multiple sport coverage of the same athlete or family.

What if weather affects outdoor events?

I monitor weather closely and communicate with families about rescheduling when necessary. Indoor makeup games are covered under the original booking when possible.

Do you offer prints?

Yes, professional quality prints in various sizes are available for order directly through your online gallery, from standard photo sizes to large wall displays.

How do usage rights work?

Families receive full personal usage rights including social media sharing, college recruitment materials, and print reproduction. Schools and organizations receive standard promotional usage rights. Extended commercial licensing is available when needed.

Response Commitment

I respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during the school year, usually much sooner during evenings and weekends. Summer responses are typically within a few hours. During busy sports seasons, I book quickly, so don't wait to reach out about important games or events you want covered.
